How to Use the Gentle Cleanser

Step 1: Wet your face with lukewarm water.

Step 2: Apply a small amount of cleanser to your hands.

Step 3: Gently massage the cleanser onto your face in circular motions.

Step 4: Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water.

Step 5: Pat your face dry with a clean towel.

How to Use the Facial Toner

Step 1: Cleanse your face thoroughly with a gentle cleanser.

Step 2: Pour a small amount of toner onto a cotton pad.

Step 3: Gently swipe the cotton pad across your face and neck.

Step 4: Allow the toner to dry before applying moisturizer or serum.

How to Use the Facial Serum

Step 1: Cleanse your face thoroughly and pat it dry.

Step 2: Apply a few drops of serum to your fingertips.

Step 3: Gently massage the serum onto your face and neck.

Step 4: Allow the serum to absorb completely into your skin.

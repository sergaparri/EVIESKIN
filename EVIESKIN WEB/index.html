CLEANSE: Gentle Facial Cleanser

Step 1: Wet face with lukewarm water.
Step 2: Drop a small amount of Gentle Facial Cleanser to your finger.
Step 3: Gently massage onto your face in circular motions.
Step 4: Wash thoroughly with lukewarm water.
Step 5: Pat your face dry with a clean towel.

REPLENISH: Facial Toner

Step 1
After cleansing face with Evie Gentle Facial Cleanser, pour a small amount of toner onto a face with cotton pad.
Step 2
Gently swipe the cotton pad across face and neck.
Step 3
Allow the toner to dry before applying the serum.

GLOW: Facial Serum

Step 1
After applying Evie Facial Toner, apply a few drops of serum to finger tips.
Step 2
Gently massage the serum onto face and neck.
Step 3
Allow the serum to absorb completely into skin.
